Q: What are the admission requirements?
A: Requirements vary by program. Generally, we require a completed application, official transcripts, standardized test scores (if applicable), and letters of recommendation.

Q: When is the application deadline?
A: The application deadline for the upcoming academic year is March 31st. We recommend applying early to be considered for scholarships.

Q: Where can I find the Academic Calendar?
A: The official Academic Calendar is available via the "View Academic Calendar" card on this dashboard and on the main college website.

Q: How do I register for courses?
A: Course registration is done online through the student portal at the beginning of each semester.

Q: What are the hostel facilities like?
A: Our hostels provide shared accommodation with essential furniture, 24/7 security, Wi-Fi access, and recreational areas.

Q: Is the Health Center open 24/7?
A: Yes, as indicated on the dashboard, the campus Health Center provides round-the-clock medical assistance for all students.

Q: How can I pay my tuition fees?
A: Fees can be paid online through the Fee Collection Module on this dashboard, via bank transfer, or at the campus accounts office.

Q: Are there any scholarships available?
A: Yes, the college offers a variety of merit-based and need-based scholarships. Information is available on the college website's 'Financial Aid' section.
